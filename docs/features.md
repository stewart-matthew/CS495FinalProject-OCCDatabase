# Features

The OCC Database includes a suite of features designed to assist the West Alabama Operation Christmas Child team in managing churches, volunteers, and events.

## Church Management

- **Search, Sort, and Filter** — Easily search and sort churches by their shoebox collection numbers for any given year.
- **Accurate Church Records** — Maintain an up-to-date list of churches, including contact information, locations, and collection data.

## Team Member Organization

- **Team Member Profiles** — Store and manage team member information, including assigned churches, contact details, and t-shirt sizes.
- **Marketing Data** — Access organized contact information for marketing and outreach efforts.

## Individual Tracking

- **Interest List Management** — Store data from individuals who have expressed interest in OCC events.
- **Search and Update** — Search, sort, and update individual records directly from the website.

## Real-Time Communication

- **Note Taking** — Add notes to churches and individuals to ensure all team members see updates in real time.
- **Collaboration** — Every team member can contribute notes, improving communication and coordination across the team.
